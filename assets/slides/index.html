What We're Building

No surveys. No eye-trackers.

We simulate virtual user behavior — clicks, dwell time, dropout points — and analyze those patterns to understand why readers drop off.

Approach
Synthetic
No IRB, no survey bias — fully reproducible simulation
Method
Telemetry
Behavioral logs: dwell time, click paths, dropout flags
Output
Dashboard
Interactive 3-tab Streamlit app deployed on Render
Timeline
1.5 Weeks
Sprint: Jul 9 – 16 · Clean, reproducible pipeline

Why This Matters

Understanding why audiences lose attention is as important as understanding what they engage with.

In the AI-driven media environment, our methodology is transferable to real-world journalism: evaluating narrative formats, identifying dropout points in long-form pieces, A/B testing information architectures.
